FAQ

What does “include end date” mean?

It counts both the start and end dates, adding one extra day to the total.

What if I enter the dates backwards?

The calculator automatically swaps them so you still get the correct difference.

Are months exact or approximate?

Months are shown as an approximate value based on 30 days.
