    When to use a stopwatch vs a timer

    Use a stopwatch when you want to measure how long something takes. Use a timer when you need a countdown to a specific end time.

    FAQ

    How do lap times work?

    Each lap records the total elapsed time plus the split since your previous lap.

    Can I use this on mobile?

    Yes. The layout is mobile-friendly and works in any modern browser.

    Will it keep running if my screen locks?

    Some devices pause background timers. Keep the screen awake for the most accurate timing.
