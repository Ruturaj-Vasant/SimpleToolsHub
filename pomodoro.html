What is the Pomodoro technique?

It’s a simple method: work in short, timed intervals, then take a short break. The rhythm helps you stay focused and avoid burnout.

When Pomodoro works best

  • Studying for exams
  • Writing or coding sprints
  • Deep work sessions
  • Breaking large tasks into chunks

FAQ

Can I customize work and break lengths?

Yes. Set your own work and break minutes before starting.

Do sessions keep counting if I switch tabs?

Yes, the timer keeps counting down. Keep the tab open to hear alerts.

Is 25/5 required?

No. Use 25/5, 50/10, or any custom durations that suit your focus style.
